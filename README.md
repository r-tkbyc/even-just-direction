# Just Direction — Prototype (Even G2)

A minimal navigator for **Even Realities G2** glasses. Pick a destination on your phone; the glasses show a start-to-goal progress bar, a compass strip, an arrow pointing toward your destination, and the remaining distance. No turn-by-turn and no map on the glasses — just the direction and how far.

> **This repository hosts the prototype / tester build**, loaded via the Even App's Prototype mode. It is not the Even Hub release.

## Try it

1. On your phone, open the **Even App** and go to **Prototype mode**.
2. Scan the QR code below (or enter the URL):
   `https://r-tkbyc.github.io/even-just-direction/`
3. Pick a destination: pan the map so the centre crosshair is on your target, then tap **Set as destination**.
4. Choose a heading mode (**Compass** or **Body Tracking**) and tap **Start**.
5. On the glasses, the arrow points toward your destination with the distance below.

Ring controls: **single tap** = open the on-glasses menu · **double tap** = exit.

![QR — even-just-direction](QR_221649.png)

## GPS

- **iPhone** — works with the built-in GPS.
- **Android** — the Even Hub in-app browser can't read GPS directly, so install the free **GPS Bridge** helper app and tap Start in it:
  https://github.com/r-tkbyc/takemotions-gps-bridge/releases
